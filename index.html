About Moonbeam

Moonbeam Cinema is a registered entertainment company based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, dedicated to bringing people together through unique movie experiences. Through regular screenings, creative venues, and a strong sense of community, we are reimagining cinema for a new generation.
